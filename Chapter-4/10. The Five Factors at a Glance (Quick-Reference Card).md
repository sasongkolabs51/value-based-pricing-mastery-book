## 10. The Five Factors at a Glance (Quick-Reference Card)

- **Standardization:** How repeatable and codified is the process? High standardization = more AI-susceptible.
- **Data Dependency:** How much of the value comes from public/structured data? High dependency opens the door for AI synthesis.
- **Judgment Requirement:** How much contextual calibration, risk-weighting, and human reassurance is needed? This is your AI shield.
- **Risk Transfer:** How much liability does your firm assume? AI cannot be sued; your signature is a premium asset.
- **Client Perception:** How does the client *currently* view the service? This measures market readiness, not objective worth.

**The composite score** tells you where a service falls on the susceptibility spectrum. The heatmap tells you what to do about it.
