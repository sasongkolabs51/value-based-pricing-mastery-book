## Template 2: Transition Heatmap Grid (Blank Plot)

**Purpose:** Visually map each service line on a two‑axis grid to identify red, yellow, green, and blue zones.

**Instructions:**  
- Draw a two‑axis grid on a large sheet of paper or in a digital drawing tool.  
- **X‑Axis (horizontal):** AI‑Susceptibility Score → 1 (left) to 5 (right).  
- **Y‑Axis (vertical):** Annual Revenue Contribution → $0 at the bottom, your highest‑revenue service at the top.  
- Plot each service line as a point, labeling it with the service line name.  
- Draw a vertical line at score 3.0 and a horizontal line at your median revenue to create four quadrants.  
- Color the quadrants:
  - **Red (top‑right):** High susceptibility, high revenue → urgent transition candidates.
  - **Yellow (bottom‑right):** High susceptibility, low revenue → automate, subscription, or divest.
  - **Green (top‑left):** Low susceptibility, high revenue → protect and expand.
  - **Blue (bottom‑left):** Low susceptibility, low revenue → niche specialties to nurture.

**Blank Grid (ASCII representation):**

```
Revenue
^
| [Green Zone]     | [Red Zone]
|                  |
|------------------|------------------- Susceptibility Score (3.0)
|                  |
| [Blue Zone]      | [Yellow Zone]
+------------------------------------------> Susceptibility (1 → 5)
```
