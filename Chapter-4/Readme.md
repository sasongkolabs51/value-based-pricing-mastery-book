# Chapter 4 Companion Workbook  
*The Susceptibility Audit – Mapping Your Entire Service Portfolio Against AI’s Attack Surface*

This workbook is the diagnostic engine of your transition. By the time you finish, you’ll have a fully scored portfolio, a strategic heatmap, and the first clear list of services to protect, transform, or let go. Work through each section sequentially. The audit demands honesty, not optimism. If you catch yourself protecting a favorite service line from a low score, that’s exactly the moment to dig deeper.
