## 6. Implementation Checklist for Chapter 4  
*Step‑by‑step to turn the chapter into a completed audit in a week.*

- [ ] **Reread Chapter 4**, highlighting the five factor descriptions and the scoring scale.  
- [ ] **Complete the Pre‑Audit Readiness Check** to gauge your starting mindset.  
- [ ] **List every service line** your practice delivers. Break them down until you can’t reasonably split further.  
- [ ] **Score each service line** using the 5‑Factor Scoring Rubric. Be honest—this data is for your eyes only.  
- [ ] **Calculate composite scores** and record revenue for each line.  
- [ ] **Build the Transition Heatmap.** Plot each service, draw the quadrant lines, and color‑code.  
- [ ] **Annotate the heatmap** with the dominant factor(s) driving each dot’s position.  
- [ ] **Extract pilot candidates** from the green and yellow zones, apply the 3‑C filter, and select your top pilot.  
- [ ] **Reflect:** What’s the biggest strategic surprise from the heatmap? Write it down—this will shape your priorities.  
- [ ] **Store your audit safely.** It’s the steering document for Chapters 5, 7, and 8.
