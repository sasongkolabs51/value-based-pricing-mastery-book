## Template 4: Quick‑Reference Scoring Rubric (from Chapter 4)

*For rapid consultation while scoring. This can be printed as a bookmark.*

| Factor                   | 1 (Highly Durable)                               | 3 (Moderate)                                       | 5 (Highly Susceptible)                     |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| **Standardization**      | Entirely bespoke, novel analysis every time      | General framework, needs significant customization | Fully templated, clear decision trees      |
| **Data Dependency**      | All insight from proprietary, non‑public sources | Mix of public data and proprietary knowledge       | All data publicly available and structured |
| **Judgment Requirement** | Irreplaceable human judgment at every stage      | Complex analysis, but rules largely defined        | Minimal judgment, purely process‑driven    |
| **Risk Transfer**        | Firm assumes full liability                      | Shared liability; firm advises, client decides     | Client bears all risk                      |
| **Client Perception**    | Clients already expect fixed‑fee / value‑based   | Clients open to discussion, default hourly         | Clients deeply skeptical, demand timesheet |
