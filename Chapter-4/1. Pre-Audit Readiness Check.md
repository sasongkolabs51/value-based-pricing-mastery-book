## 1. Pre-Audit Readiness Check

Before you score, check your mindset. The audit only works if you’re willing to see your practice as it is, not as you’ve always described it. Answer honestly.

| Statement | Yes | Somewhat | No |
|-----------|:--:|:--------:|:--:|
| I’m ready to list every service I provide, even the small ones I rarely mention. | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ |
| I accept that some services I’m proud of may be highly susceptible to AI commoditization. | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ |
| I can distinguish between “this is complex” and “this requires irreplaceable human judgment.” | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ |
| I understand that the audit is not a verdict on my worth—it’s a map for strategic action. | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ |
| I’m willing to ask a trusted peer to review my scores if I suspect bias. | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ |

If you marked “No” on more than one, pause and revisit the Value Architect identity from Chapter 2. The audit requires the separation of your self-worth from any individual service line. You are not what you score.
