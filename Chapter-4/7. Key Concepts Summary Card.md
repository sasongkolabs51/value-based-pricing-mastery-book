*The chapter’s core framework in one glance.*

**THE FIVE FACTORS**  
1. **Standardization** – How templated and repeatable is the work?  
2. **Data Dependency** – Does the insight come from public, ingestible data?  
3. **Judgment Requirement** – Does it demand contextual calibration, risk-weighting, and human reassurance?  
4. **Risk Transfer** – How much liability does your firm absorb?  
5. **Client Perception** – How ready are your clients to pay for outcomes, not hours?

**SCORING SCALE (1–5)**  
1 = Highly Durable (low AI threat)  
5 = Highly Susceptible (high AI threat)

**COMPOSITE SCORE**  
A weighted average (or simple mean) that shows overall exposure. Lower scores = stronger pricing foundation.

**TRANSITION HEATMAP**  
- **Red (High Susceptibility, High Revenue):** Urgent transition to new models.  
- **Yellow (High Susceptibility, Low Revenue):** Automate, bundle, or divest.  
- **Green (Low Susceptibility, High Revenue):** Protect and expand with value pricing.  
- **Blue (Low Susceptibility, Low Revenue):** Nurture strategically.
