## 12. VRR Quick-Reference Card

**The Core Formula:**  
VRR Fee = (Outcome Significance × Risk Absorption) × Judgment Intensity

- **OS:** The dollar value at stake for the client (range, use midpoint)  
- **RA:** The % of that value you are underwriting (1–5% typical, depending on liability)  
- **JI:** A multiplier (0.4–1.0) reflecting how irreplaceable your judgment is, derived from audit scores  

**Benchmarks:**  
- VRR Fee is typically 3–10× the old hourly estimate for high-stakes work  
- VRR Fee should be 1–5% of the at-stake value  

**The Only Script You Need Internally:**  
*“The client isn’t paying for the time. They’re paying for the certainty that a multi-million-dollar risk won’t land on their desk. My fee reflects a fraction of that risk—and my judgment is what makes the risk transfer real.”*

**Client Conversation Anchor:**  
*“If this goes wrong, what does it cost you? Our fee is a small fraction of that number, and we stand behind it.”*
