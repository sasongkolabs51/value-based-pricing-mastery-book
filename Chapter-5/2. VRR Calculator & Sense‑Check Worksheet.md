### Step 2: Risk Absorption (RA)

**What proportion of the at-risk value is your firm effectively underwriting?**  
Consider your professional liability, reputational exposure, and the fact that if your work fails, the client will hold you accountable. Use the following guide:

- **1% (Low risk transfer):** Advisory work where the client retains ultimate decision-making and liability; your role is guidance.  
- **2–3% (Medium risk transfer):** You share liability; a mistake would cause significant client harm and likely trigger a claim or reputational damage.  
- **4–5% (High risk transfer):** Your firm is essentially the guarantor; an error would result in direct financial loss to the client for which you are clearly liable.
