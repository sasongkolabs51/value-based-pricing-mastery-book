*Frame the engagement around the client’s strategic objective and the risk you’re transferring.*  
*Template:* “Based on your projections, [client’s objective] represents $[OS Midpoint] in [value at stake / revenue at risk], and [primary gating risk] is the single biggest threat. Our engagement is designed to transfer that [specific risk] from your balance sheet to ours.”
