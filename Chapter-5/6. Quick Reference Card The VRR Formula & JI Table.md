## 6. Quick Reference Card: The VRR Formula & JI Table  
*Keep this at your desk for rapid mental checks.*

---

**VRR Fee = (OS Midpoint × RA) × JI**

- **OS** = Outcome Significance (dollar value at stake for the client, expressed as a range midpoint)  
- **RA** = Risk Absorption (1–5%, the portion of the at-risk value your firm is underwriting)  
- **JI** = Judgment Intensity multiplier, derived from Chapter 4 audit scores.

**JI Multiplier Lookup:**

| Avg. Judgment & Risk Transfer Score | JI |
|-------------------------------------|----|
| 1.0 – 2.0 | 1.0 |
| 2.1 – 3.0 | 0.8 |
| 3.1 – 4.0 | 0.6 |
| 4.1 – 5.0 | 0.4 |

**Golden rule:** If the average is above 4, VRR is likely not appropriate. Use automation, subscription, or divestiture strategies instead.
