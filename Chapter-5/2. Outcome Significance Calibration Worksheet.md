## 2. Outcome Significance Calibration Worksheet

*Purpose: Translate a specific client engagement into a dollar range of value at stake (OS).*

Choose one engagement from your heatmap’s green or yellow zone. Fill this out as completely as you can before talking to the client—you’ll refine it collaboratively later.

**Engagement name:** _______________________________  
**Client:** _______________________________  
**Brief description of the service:** _______________________________  

**Step 1: Identify the business value at stake.**  
What is the client trying to achieve or avoid?  
______________________________________________________  
______________________________________________________  

**Step 2: Quantify in dollar terms (or clear metrics).**  
Use these prompts. Enter a number even if it’s a range.

- If this project succeeds, what revenue is unlocked, cost saved, or investment enabled?  
  $_______________  
- If this project fails or is delayed, what is the financial impact (penalty, lost revenue, write-off, market share loss)?  
  $_______________  
- What is the total deal size, portfolio value, or exposure that this engagement protects?  
  $_______________  

**Step 3: Determine the at-risk value (Outcome Significance range).**  
Combine the above into a range: the minimum plausible value of the outcome, and the maximum. Choose a midpoint.

- Low estimate (conservative): $_______________  
- High estimate (optimistic but defensible): $_______________  
- Midpoint (use for initial VRR calculation): $_______________  

**Step 4: Source the data.**  
Where did these numbers come from?  
- [ ] Client’s own projections / board deck / investor materials  
- [ ] Public filings or industry benchmarks  
- [ ] My best informed estimate based on similar engagements  
- [ ] Actual conversation with the client  

*If you checked only “my best estimate,” plan a conversation to validate the OS with the client. The engine is only as credible as the OS number both parties believe.*

**Example:**  
For Eleanor’s pharmaceutical market entry review, she estimated:  
- Revenue at stake if all three markets launch on time: $200M first-year  
- Risk of regulatory delay causing 12-month postponement: $80M NPV loss  
- Outcome Significance range: $50–80M (midpoint $65M)  
- Source: client’s board presentation on expansion strategy.
