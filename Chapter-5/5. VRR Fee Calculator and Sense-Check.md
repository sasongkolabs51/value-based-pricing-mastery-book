**Sense-check against benchmarks:**  

1. Old hourly estimate: What would you have billed for this work at your pre-AI hourly rates and expected hours?  
   $_______________  

2. Ratio of VRR Fee to hourly estimate:  
   VRR ÷ Hourly = _______ : 1  
   *For high-stakes, judgment-heavy work, 3:1 to 10:1 is common. If below 2:1, your OS or RA may be too conservative. If above 15:1, your OS might be inflated.*  

3. VRR Fee as a percentage of OS Midpoint:  
   (VRR Fee ÷ OS Midpoint) × 100 = ______%  
   *Typically 1%–5%. The client is getting a bargain compared to the downside risk.*  

**Reality check questions:**  
- If the client saw this number, would they immediately compare it to the at-risk value, or would they compare it to their old legal/compliance budget?  
- Can I say this number out loud without flinching? If not, revisit your JI. Your discomfort may be the billable-hour anchor, not a genuine overprice.  
- Do I have at least one Disaster Portfolio story (Chapter 2) that illustrates the kind of outcome this fee protects against?

**If the number feels off, adjust the OS range or RA band (document why), and recalculate.**
