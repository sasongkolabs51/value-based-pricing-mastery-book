## Worksheet 2: Proposal Language Construction Template

**Purpose:** Convert your VRR calculation into a one‑page client proposal that embodies the engine’s logic without exposing the math. Fill in the bracketed prompts, then read the entire proposal aloud.

**The Outcome Hook**  
"Based on your projections, [insert client strategic objective, e.g., successful market entry] represents $[OS Midpoint] in [revenue at risk / value protected], and [insert primary gating risk, e.g., regulatory clearance] is the primary threat. Our engagement is designed to transfer that [specific risk] from your balance sheet to ours."

**The Success Criterion**  
"Success is defined as [insert verifiable outcome from your Chapter 6 packaging, e.g., delivery of fully compliant documentation packages], with zero material deficiencies."

**The Fixed Fee**  
"The professional fee for this outcome‑based engagement is $[VRR Fee]. This fee covers the complete delivery of the defined outcome and the assumption of the associated [regulatory/reputational] risk. No additional charges will apply unless the scope of this outcome changes by mutual written agreement."

**Value Assurance (select one or adapt)**  
- Simple: "If the defined success criterion is not met due to our error, we will remediate the deficiency at our own cost and credit [X]% of the professional fee."
- Risk‑Sharing: "We propose a base fee of $[X] and a success fee equal to [X]% of the [savings/penalty reduction] achieved beyond the baseline."
