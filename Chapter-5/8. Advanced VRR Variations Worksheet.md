RA: ______%  

**Step 3: Determine blended JI.**  
Average judgment/risk scores across the portfolio.  
JI: ______  

**Step 4: Calculate annual VRR base.**  
Annual VRR = (OS annual × RA) × JI = $_______________  

**Step 5: Convert to periodic retainer.**  
Monthly retainer: $_______________

**Proposal language snippet:**  
*“Our annual retainer of $[X] reflects the aggregate regulatory and transaction risk we manage across all your matters. It provides you with a fixed, predictable cost for certainty across your entire portfolio.”*

### B. Performance-Contingent VRR

**Base fee (minimum economics):** $_______________  
*Justify this with the risk absorption you provide regardless of outcome.*  

**Success metric:** ____________________________________  
*Example: penalty reduction below $5M threshold, deal closure before deadline, license approval without conditions.*  

**Success fee:** $_______________ or ______% of incremental value  
*Example: 10% of penalty reduction below the agreed threshold.*  

**VRR structure:**  
*“Base fee: $X, covering our minimum commitment and risk assumption. Success fee: $Y if [metric]. This aligns our interests: we succeed only when you do.”*
