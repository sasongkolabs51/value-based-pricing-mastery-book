## Template 2: Invoice Reframe Drill Worksheet

**Purpose:** Prime your brain to spontaneously offer a value story instead of defending a timesheet.

**Step 1 – Select a recent invoice** that a client questioned, or one you worry they might question.  
Invoice/Engagement: [Brief description]

**Step 2 – Draft a one‑page “value narrative”** explaining the fee entirely through the business outcome delivered and the risk avoided. Use zero time references.  
[Write your narrative here. Focus on the specific problem solved, the risk neutralized, and the tangible result for the client’s business.]

**Step 3 – Practice aloud.** Deliver this narrative as if you were standing in the client’s office. Record yourself or rehearse with a colleague.  
After practicing, note:  
- Where did I naturally emphasize value?  
- Where did I feel the pull to mention hours, and how did I reframe it?
