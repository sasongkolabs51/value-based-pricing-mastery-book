## 5. Procurement Pushback Pivot Script Builder  
*Purpose: Replace the defensive reflex with a precise, outcome-based response you can deliver naturally.*

**Step 1: Recall a specific recent objection** from a client or prospect about time, AI speed, or efficiency. Write exactly what they said.

---

**Step 2: Write down your old, instinctive defensive reaction.** What did you feel like saying (or actually say)? Even if it was just internal, capture it.

---

**Step 3: Craft your three-sentence pivot.**  
The rule: **no mention of “time,” “hours,” “effort,” “speed,” or “how long.”** Only business risk and certainty. Structure it as:  
- Sentence 1: Acknowledge the outcome they care about.  
- Sentence 2: Name the risk you mitigated (or the upside you secured).  
- Sentence 3: Connect that risk to the fee, reframing cost as insurance or investment.

*Example: “You’re right, the technology handles the drafting. But what you’re really paying for is the judgment that kept your deal structure out of the regulatory crosshairs—a misstep there would have cost you over $2 million in penalties. This fee secures that certainty.”*
