## 3. Invoice Reframe Drill Template

Pick a recent invoice you either had to defend or one you quietly worried might be challenged. Use this template to craft a one-page “value narrative” that never mentions time, hourly rates, or effort.

**The situation (1-2 sentences):**  
*What was the client facing before your work?*

**The outcome delivered:**  
*Describe in business terms what changed because of your work. Examples: a deal closed, a penalty avoided, a system stabilized, a board decision secured.*

**The risk avoided:**  
*What was the alternative scenario? What stood to be lost? Quantify it—money, market position, litigation exposure, reputational damage.*

**Tangible value created or preserved (quantify as much as possible):**  
*Put a number to it, even if approximate. $X million in avoided fines; Y% reduction in compliance cycle time; Z basis points of shareholder value protected.*

**Why the fee is aligned with that value:**  
*Here’s where you connect the outcome to your fee. Use language like, “The certainty this provided against a known $20 million exposure made a fee of $50,000 an asymmetric investment in risk reduction.” No time logic allowed.*

Now practice delivering this narrative out loud, as if you were sitting across from the client’s CFO. Do it until the rhythm feels natural, not defensive.
