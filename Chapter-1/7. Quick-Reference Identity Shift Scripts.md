## 7. Quick-Reference Identity Shift Scripts

The old billable-hour voice will return in moments of stress. Keep these scripts handy—on a sticky note, in your phone, or in the back of your notebook. When you catch yourself slipping, repeat the relevant one.

- *“The value I delivered today isn’t in my timesheet.”*  
- *“Speed is now a feature of my expertise, not a discount.”*  
- *“I charge for the destination, not the ride.”*  
- *“AI handled the process; I handled the judgment. That’s what the client pays for.”*  
- *“If this were easy to replicate, the client wouldn’t need me.”*  
- *“My worth is the risk I removed, not the time I spent.”*  
- *“Clients don’t want hours; they want certainty. I sell certainty.”*

Pick the one that resonates most. Use it as a mantra when you open your laptop or before a difficult client meeting.
