## 7. Key Mental Shifts Summary Card  
*Cut out and keep handy—a quick reference for when the old voice returns.*

---

**SHIFT 1: From “time is currency” → “outcome is currency.”**  
Surgeons charge for restored health, not minutes in surgery. You charge for business certainty, not tenth-of-an-hour slices.

**SHIFT 2: From “AI stole my value” → “AI exposed where my real value lives.”**  
The parts AI drafts were never your differentiator. Your judgment, risk assessment, and strategic framing are now unmistakable.

**SHIFT 3: From “career measured in hours” → “career measured in impact.”**  
Your autobiography is a portfolio of mergers saved, penalties avoided, and markets pivoted—not a ledger of minutes.

**SHIFT 4: From “trusted advisor who bills” → “Value Architect who prices outcomes.”**  
You design commercial relationships around results and risks, not time units. That’s a different role entirely.

---

**Emergency reminder:** When anxiety spikes, ask yourself: *What outcome did I create, and what was the cost of not having that outcome?* That’s your fee justification.
