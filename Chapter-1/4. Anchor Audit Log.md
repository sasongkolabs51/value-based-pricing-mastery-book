## 4. Anchor Audit Log  
*Purpose: Drag your invisible billable-hour programming into the light and rewrite it moment by moment.*

For **three consecutive working days**, carry this log (or a digital version). Every time you catch yourself measuring your worth in hours logged, utilization rates, or time spent, make a note. Immediately reframe that same thought in outcome language, using the columns below.
