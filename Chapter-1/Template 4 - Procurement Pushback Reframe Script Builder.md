## Template 4: Procurement Pushback Reframe Script Builder

**Purpose:** Replace the defensive reflex when a client objects about time/AI with a strategic, risk‑centered response.

**Step 1 – Recall a specific recent objection** (e.g., “How do I know I’m not overpaying if this only took a week?”).  
[Describe the objection exactly.]

**Step 2 – Draft a three‑sentence verbal pivot** that shifts the conversation from “hours spent” to “risk mitigated,” without using the word “time” once.

- Sentence 1 (Acknowledge & redirect): [e.g., “I understand the concern—what matters is the business outcome we delivered.”]
- Sentence 2 (Specific risk/value): [e.g., “By catching the regulatory gap early, we protected you from an estimated $5M in potential fines.”]
- Sentence 3 (Invite to outcome evaluation): [e.g., “Can we review the result together against the success criteria we defined upfront?”]

**Step 3 – Practice** until the delivery feels natural and unrehearsed. Role‑play with a colleague who plays the skeptical client.
