**Step 2: Rewrite each one.**  
Now go back and completely rewrite each achievement. **Erase every reference to time spent, team size, hours billed, or effort.** Use only the language of client business impact and risk averted. For each, answer these three questions:

- *What was the dollar value of the outcome?* (Be specific—estimate if you have to, but anchor in real numbers the client would recognize.)  
- *What risk was neutralized?* (Litigation avoided, regulatory penalty, market loss, reputational damage, etc.)  
- *How would the client have suffered without this result?* (Describe the alternate future you prevented.)
