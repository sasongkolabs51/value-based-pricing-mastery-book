For each achievement, answer these questions (do this mentally or in a journal):

- **Dollar Value of the Outcome:** What revenue was protected, what cost was avoided, what funding was unlocked? If you can’t pinpoint an exact number, give a credible range.  
- **Risk Neutralized:** What regulatory, reputational, operational, or strategic risk did you remove? What was the probability and impact of that risk materializing without your intervention?  
- **Client Impact Without It:** If you had not done this work, what would the client’s world look like today? Be specific.

*Reflection:* Now read your five Outcome Rewrites aloud. Which version—the original memory or the rewritten one—feels more accurate about your contribution? Which one would you rather be known for?
