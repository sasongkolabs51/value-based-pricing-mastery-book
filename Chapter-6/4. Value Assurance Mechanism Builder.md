## 4. Value Assurance Mechanism Builder  
*Purpose: Draft a contractual clause that makes the client feel protected—and makes the fee self‑justifying. Choose the mechanism type that fits your engagement, then fill in the template.*

**Select your mechanism type:**  
- [ ] **Fixed Fee with Scope Change Provision** (simplest, works for most)  
- [ ] **Success Fee / Risk‑Sharing** (part of fee tied to a defined metric, like penalty reduction)  
- [ ] **Outcome Guarantee** (portion of fee refunded or credited if outcome not met, use sparingly)

---

### Template A: Fixed Fee with Scope Change Provision  
*“The professional fee of $[amount] covers the complete delivery of the defined success criterion. No additional charges will apply unless the scope of this outcome changes materially by mutual written agreement. If the client requests services outside the defined outcome, we will provide a separate fixed‑price proposal before commencing that work.”*

### Template B: Success Fee / Risk‑Sharing  
*Base fee:* $[amount]  
*Success metric:* [e.g., reduction in penalty amount below $X]  
*Success fee:* [e.g., 15% of the amount by which the actual penalty falls below the baseline]  

*Draft clause:*  
*“The base professional fee of $[base] covers our risk assumption and minimum economics irrespective of outcome. In addition, if [success metric] is achieved, the client will pay a success fee equal to [formula]. The total fee will not exceed $[cap, if any]. This structure directly aligns our interests with the client’s goal of minimizing [specific risk].”*
