## 3. Archetype Selection Matrix Quick‑Reference  
*Use this matrix to classify your service and choose the right commercial package. Match your Chapter 4 audit scores to the descriptions.*

| Archetype | Best Fit | Dominant Audit Factor | Outcome Statement Focus | Fee Structure | Risk Allocation Language |
|-----------|----------|----------------------|-------------------------|---------------|--------------------------|
| **Assurance Package** | Audit opinions, regulatory certifications, compliance sign‑offs, internal control assessments | High Risk Transfer score (1–2, durable) | Avoiding a defined harm; compliance certainty | Fixed fee, heavily weighted on RA in VRR | “We assume responsibility for the accuracy… If a material deficiency arises from our error, we remediate at our cost.” |
| **Advisory Package** | Market entry strategy, M&A target assessment, board governance advice, strategic roadmap | High Judgment Requirement score (1–2, durable) | Quality and timeliness of the recommendation; decision confidence | Fixed fee or base + performance bonus linked to decision result; JI multiplier drives VRR | “Our recommendation draws on our analysis and experience… Historically, this has reduced the probability of [bad outcome] by approximately X%.” |
| **Assurance‑Plus‑Advisory** | Both a compliance deliverable and strategic interpretation, e.g., tax opinion with strategic planning, regulatory filing with board‑level counsel | High Judgment and Risk Transfer scores (both 1–2) | Dual: compliance outcome + strategic insight | Highest fee, reflecting dual value streams; VRR can use composite weighting | Combines both language types: “We assume responsibility for the certification, and our strategic recommendations are based on the same standard of care.” |

**Action:** For each engagement you plan to package, circle the dominant archetype and let it guide your fee structure and risk language.
