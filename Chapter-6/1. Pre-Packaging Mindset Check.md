## 1. Pre-Packaging Mindset Check

Before you write a word, check what’s still running in the background. Tick any statement that feels true right now.

- [ ] When I draft an engagement letter, my first instinct is still to list all the tasks the team will perform.
- [ ] I’m more comfortable describing *how* we’ll work than *what* the client will have at the end.
- [ ] Somewhere in the back of my mind, I believe the client deserves to see an hourly breakdown if they ask—it’s just fair.
- [ ] I worry that if the scope is too focused on outcomes, the client might hold me responsible for things outside my control.
- [ ] I haven’t yet shown a client an engagement letter that’s free of all time-related clauses.
- [ ] I’m not sure how to talk about AI’s role in delivery without triggering a fee objection.

If you ticked any of these, you’re holding a perfectly normal set of billable-hour instincts. This chapter exists to give you documents so clear and client-aligned that those instincts lose their grip.
