## 10. Key Concepts Summary Card  
*The packaging playbook’s essentials in a single glance.*

---

**THE OUTCOME STATEMENT**  
- Business Objective + Verifiable Success Criterion + Quantified Value/Risk Protected.  
- Replaces all task lists. If you can’t tell whether the engagement succeeded from this paragraph alone, rewrite it.

**THREE ARCHETYPES**  
- **Assurance:** You provide a certification and absorb liability. Fee driven by Risk Absorption.  
- **Advisory:** You provide strategic judgment. Fee driven by Judgment Intensity; may include performance bonus.  
- **Assurance‑Plus‑Advisory:** Both. Highest value, highest fee.

**VALUE ASSURANCE MECHANISMS**  
- Fixed Fee + Scope Change Provision (universal default).  
- Success Fee / Risk‑Sharing (aligns incentives).  
- Outcome Guarantee (use only when you control the outcome).  
- Keep it simple—one paragraph max.

**RISK ALLOCATION LANGUAGE**  
- Assurance: “We assume responsibility for accuracy; if we err, we fix it at our cost.”  
- Advisory: “Our advice, based on experience, reduces the probability of [bad outcome] by X%.”  
- Include AI statement: “We review, validate, and assume full responsibility for AI‑generated output.”

**HOURLY‑LANGUAGE PURGE**  
- No hourly breakdowns, time records, rate cards, or clauses that tie fee to hours.  
- If you can’t remove, supersede via side letter.

**PROCUREMENT PUSHBACK**  
- Don’t defend hours—pivot to the risk you absorb and the outcome you guarantee.  
- Offer milestone tracking instead of time reports.
