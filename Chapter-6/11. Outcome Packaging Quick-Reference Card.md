## 11. Outcome Packaging Quick-Reference Card

**The Four Layers of an Outcome Package**
1. **Outcome Statement:** Business context, success criterion, quantified value.
2. **Archetype:** Assurance (risk absorption), Advisory (strategic judgment), or Assurance-Plus-Advisory (both).
3. **Value Assurance Mechanism:** Fixed fee with no surprises, success fee, or outcome guarantee.
4. **Risk Allocation Language:** Visible liability assumption and AI disclosure.

**Page One Must Answer:**
- What does success look like?
- What does it cost?
- What happens if the outcome isn’t met?

**The Golden Rule:** If the client can still ask “how many hours?” after reading page one, the package isn’t done. Remove every time-shaped handle.

**Conversation Anchor:** “We’re pricing the result, not the ride.”
