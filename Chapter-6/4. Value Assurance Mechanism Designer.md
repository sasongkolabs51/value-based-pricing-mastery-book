## 4. Value Assurance Mechanism Designer

The mechanism removes the client’s fear of overpaying. It answers the unspoken question: *“What if it takes you less effort than I’m paying for—or what if the outcome isn’t exactly what you promised?”*

### Menu of Mechanisms

**A. “Agreed Outcome, Fixed Fee, No Surprises”** (simplest, use for most first packages)  
- The fee covers the defined outcome.  
- No additional charges unless the scope changes by mutual written agreement.  
- Client knows the price upfront and never sees a timesheet.

**B. Success Fee / Performance Bonus** (adds upside alignment)  
- Base fee covers minimum economics and risk absorption.  
- Additional fee if a defined stretch metric is achieved (e.g., penalty reduced below $X, deal closed by Y date, license granted without conditions).  
- Success fee expressed as a percentage of the incremental value created or a flat bonus.

**C. Outcome Guarantee** (strongest trust signal, use selectively)  
- A portion of the fee is refundable or credited if the defined outcome is not met, provided the failure is within your control.  
- Requires tight definition of what is within your control and clear carve-outs.
