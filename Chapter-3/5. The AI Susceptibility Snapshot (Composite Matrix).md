### The AI Susceptibility Matrix

Plot each component from the tables onto this grid. The horizontal axis is Replaceability (1–5, left to right); the vertical axis is Judgment Intensity (1–5, bottom to top). This creates four quadrants:

```
High Judgment (5) |   Stronghold     |   Oversight Zone
                  |   (Low Replace,  |   (Mod Replace,
                  |    High Judgment)|    High Judgment)
                  |------------------|------------------
                  |   Mixed Utility  |   Susceptible
                  |   (Low Replace,  |   (High Replace,
                  |    Low Judgment) |    Low Judgment)
Low Judgment  (1) |__________________|__________________
                  1                                  5
                      Replaceability (AI-able)

Low Replaceability = hard for AI to replicate (left side)
High Replaceability = easy for AI to replicate (right side)
```

- **Stronghold (High Judgment, Low Replaceability):** Your irreplaceable core. Protect with value pricing.
- **Oversight Zone (High Judgment, High Replaceability):** AI can draft the baseline, but your oversight and signature are essential. Price for risk oversight, not drafting.
- **Mixed Utility (Low Judgment, Low Replaceability):** Specialized but not judgment-heavy; consider automating or bundling into a subscription.
- **Susceptible (Low Judgment, High Replaceability):** Pure process. Automate aggressively or move to an efficiency-based fee. Do not let these items anchor your value.
