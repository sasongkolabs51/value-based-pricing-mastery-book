- **Lower Right (AI‑Susceptible):** Automate, productize, or subscription‑price with urgency.  
- **Upper Right (Mixed):** Your safe‑zone for value pricing pilots.  
- **Lower Left (Commodity Plus):** Small, bespoke, low‑stakes—manage but don’t obsess.
