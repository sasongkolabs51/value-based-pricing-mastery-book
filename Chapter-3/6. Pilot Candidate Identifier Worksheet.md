**Step 2: Apply the “3‑C Filter” (preview of Chapter 7’s selection criteria):**  
Score each candidate 1‑5 on the three C’s.

- **Complexity (low is better):** Can this outcome be clearly defined and scoped without ambiguity? (1 = crystal clear, 5 = highly ambiguous)  
- **Client openness (high is better):** How receptive is this specific client to non‑hourly arrangements? Have they expressed frustration with traditional billing or shown interest in fixed fees? (1 = resistant, 5 = enthusiastic)  
- **Containable scope (high is better):** Can you ring‑fence this component so that if it goes sideways, it doesn’t bleed into the rest of the relationship? (1 = entangled, 5 = fully independent)
