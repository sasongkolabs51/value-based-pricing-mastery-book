# Chapter 3 Companion Workbook  
*The AI Exposure Scan – Turning Your Biggest Threat into Your Sharpest Diagnostic*

This workbook converts the three mental lenses from Chapter 3 into a permanent diagnostic reflex. By the time you finish, you’ll have a granular map of your practice’s vulnerability to AI—and a clear pilot candidate to move on. Work through each tool sequentially. The goal isn’t to eliminate AI anxiety entirely; it’s to replace the fog of panic with a precise set of coordinates you can act on.
