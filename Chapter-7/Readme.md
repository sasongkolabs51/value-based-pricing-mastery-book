# The Pilot Protocol Companion Toolkit  
*Practical templates and guided exercises for Chapter 7*

You’ve built the audit, the engine, and the outcome packages. Now you need to test them in the real world without betting your career on a single roll of the dice. This toolkit gives you everything you need to select, design, run, and learn from your first value‑pricing pilot—safely, systematically, and with a clear path to proof. Work through each section in order. By the end, you’ll have a complete pilot blueprint, a conversation script, and a feedback system that turns a single engagement into the evidence you need to scale.
