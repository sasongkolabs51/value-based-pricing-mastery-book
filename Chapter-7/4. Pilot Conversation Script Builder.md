### Common Client Questions and Your Prepared Answers

**Q: “What if it takes you less time than you thought? Am I overpaying?”**  
*A:* “If we deliver the outcome faster, that’s a win for both of us—you get the result sooner, and we’re incentivized to be efficient. The fee reflects the value of the outcome, not the hours underneath. Think of it like an insurance premium: you’re paying for the removal of a specific risk, not the minutes it took to draft the policy.”

**Q: “Can I see an hourly backup just for my records?”**  
*A:* “I’d prefer we judge the engagement by the outcome on page one. If we’re successful, the hours won’t matter; if we’re not, we have the assurance mechanism to address it. But if your internal system absolutely requires a number, we can provide a good-faith estimate of the resource commitment—not as a billing basis, just as context.”

**Q: “What if the scope changes mid-project?”**  
*A:* “We’ll handle it transparently. If something new comes up, we pause, define the new outcome, and agree on a revised fee before we proceed. No hidden charges, no creeping bill.”
