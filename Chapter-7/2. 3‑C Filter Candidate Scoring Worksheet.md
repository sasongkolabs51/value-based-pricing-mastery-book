**Step 2: Score each candidate on the 3 C’s.**  
Use a 1–5 scale for each, but note the direction:

- **Complexity:** 1 = extremely complex (many interdependencies, hard to define outcome), 5 = very simple (clear outcome, well‑understood path). *Aim for high scores.*  
- **Client Openness:** 1 = deeply skeptical, likely to resist; 5 = already interested in alternative fees, highly trusting, has experimented before. *Aim for high scores.*  
- **Containability:** 1 = open‑ended, scope likely to drift, long timeline; 5 = tightly bounded, short duration, single clear deliverable. *Aim for high scores.*
