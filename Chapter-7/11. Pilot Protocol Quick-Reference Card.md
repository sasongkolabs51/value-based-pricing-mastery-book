## 11. Pilot Protocol Quick-Reference Card

**The 3-C Filter:**  
- Low Complexity – you know the path.  
- High Client Openness – they trust you and will talk openly.  
- Containable Scope – bounded in time and deliverables.

**The Blueprint (one page):**  
Outcome + VRR Fee + Old-Model Comparison + Conversation Prompts + Milestone Plan.

**The Feedback Loop:**  
- After initial conversation  
- At each milestone check-in (ask about the model)  
- Formal debrief (three standard questions)

**The Only Metric That Matters (Early On):**  
Not fee magnitude. Not margin. But: *Did the client pay the agreed fee without demanding an hourly breakdown, and are they willing to do it again?*

**The Mindset:**  
This is a controlled experiment, not a bet-the-firm launch. Protect the relationship, capture the data, refine the model. The pilot is your lab, not your legacy.
