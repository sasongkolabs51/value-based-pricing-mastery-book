### Part B: Post-Pyramid Redesign (The Canvas)

Now rebuild the workflow using three functional layers, not seniority levels. The goal is to minimize the time from outcome definition to certified deliverable while increasing client touchpoints and margin.

**Layer 1: Strategic Oversight (Partner)**  
*What you’ll do:*
- Define the outcome with the client and structure the engagement (price, risk, scope).
- Conduct the retraining conversation and set expectations.
- Certify the final deliverable and assume liability.
- Escalate any judgment calls that the AI-Augmented Analysis layer flags.
- Estimated partner hours per engagement: ______ (target: significantly lower than old model).

**Layer 2: AI-Augmented Analysis (Senior Professionals + Technology)**  
*What this layer does:*
- Orchestrate AI tools: prompt, validate, and refine outputs.
- Perform the judgment-intensive synthesis—applying contextual calibration, risk-weighting, and industry knowledge that AI can’t replicate.
- Prepare a “partner-ready” synthesis with clear flags where human judgment overrides AI recommendations.
- Roles assigned: _______________________________  
- Estimated hours per engagement: ______

**Layer 3: Client-Embedded Validation (Client-Facing Professional)**  
*What this layer does:*
- Serve as the client’s day-to-day contact during the engagement.
- Ensure the outcome is being implemented correctly on the client side.
- Capture real-time feedback and surface new outcome-based opportunities.
- Conduct milestone check-ins and collect data for the pilot feedback loop.
- Roles assigned: _______________________________  
- Estimated hours per engagement: ______ (may be lower initially, grows as model matures)

**Post-Pyramid Workflow Diagram (sketch or list the new sequence):**  
1. (Strategic Oversight) Outcome definition, pricing, and conversation  
2. (AI-Augmented Analysis) Initial AI orchestration and data gathering  
3. (Client-Embedded) First client check-in to validate direction  
4. (AI-Augmented Analysis) Deep analysis and judgment overlay  
5. (Strategic Oversight) Mid-point review and escalation decisions  
6. (Client-Embedded) Draft results walkthrough with client  
7. (AI-Augmented Analysis) Final refinement based on client input  
8. (Strategic Oversight) Final certification and delivery  
9. (Client-Embedded) Post-delivery debrief and future-pacing  

**Key change:** Hours shift from partners doing review and juniors doing data gathering to AI-Augmented Analysis as the core engine, with partner time concentrated on certification and high-stakes judgment. Client contact is continuous, not just at endpoints.

**Link to Your Audit:**  
- If your audit shows high standardization and data dependency, the AI-Augmented Analysis layer does the heavy lifting.  
- If judgment intensity is high, the partner’s role in Step 5 is critical and the JI multiplier in your VRR engine remains high.  
- Risk transfer lives in the partner’s certification (Step 8).
