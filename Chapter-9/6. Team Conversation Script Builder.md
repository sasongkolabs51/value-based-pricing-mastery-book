## 6. Team Conversation Script Builder  
*Purpose: Communicate the delivery model changes to your team in a way that addresses their fears and builds buy‑in. This is not a one‑time announcement; it’s the start of an ongoing dialogue.*

**Audience:** The professionals who will move into AI‑Augmented Analysis or Client‑Embedded Validation roles, or who are currently anxious about AI’s impact on their billable hours.

**Opening frame (acknowledge the reality):**  
*“I want to talk openly about something many of you have probably been thinking: the way we’ve always measured performance—billable hours—is becoming a worse and worse reflection of the value you actually create. AI can do in minutes what used to take days, and if we keep evaluating you on hours, we’re going to penalize you for being efficient. That’s not fair, and it’s not smart. So we’re going to try something different.”*
