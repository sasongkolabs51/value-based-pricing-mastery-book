## 9. Key Concepts Summary Card  
*The post‑pyramid delivery model in a single glance.*

---

**THE THREE POST‑PYRAMID LAYERS**  
- **Strategic Oversight (Partner):** Outcome definition, risk calibration, final certification, client conversations. Compensated on outcome value originated and realization rate.  
- **AI‑Augmented Analysis (Experienced professionals):** AI prompting, output validation, judgment overlay. Compensated on throughput velocity, AI accuracy, and judgment call quality.  
- **Client‑Embedded Validation (Relationship‑strong professionals):** Continuous client contact, implementation tracking, scope monitoring. Compensated on outcome realization, client satisfaction, and new outcome‑based leads generated.

**THE HUMAN‑IN‑THE‑LOOP QUALITY PROTOCOL**  
1. **CP1: AI Output Validation** – Factual accuracy, completeness, context.  
2. **CP2: Judgment Overlay** – Contextual calibration, edge cases, rationale documented.  
3. **CP3: Partner Certification** – Final sign‑off, liability assumption attached.

**COMPENSATION PRINCIPLE**  
- Preserve base salary. Add variable component tied to new metrics. Fund it from the margin uplift of the post‑pyramid model. Never make someone choose between their bonus and AI efficiency.

**MARGIN FORMULA**  
- New Margin = VRR Fee – (much lower) Post‑Pyramid Delivery Cost.  
- The spread is the profit that heals the broken leverage model.

**THE GOLDEN RULE**  
*Your external pricing model will collapse if your internal delivery model still rewards billable hours. Redesign them together, or watch them tear each other apart.*

When you get this right, your team will stop resisting the transition and start championing it.
