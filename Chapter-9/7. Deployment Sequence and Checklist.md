## 7. Deployment Sequence and Checklist

Follow this sequence to roll out the post-pyramid model for your pilot service line.

**Phase 1: Workflow Redesign (Week 1–2)**  
- [ ] Complete the Current vs. Post-Pyramid Workflow Mapping (Section 2).  
- [ ] Identify which team members will fill each post-pyramid layer.  
- [ ] Draft role cards with new metrics (Section 3).  
- [ ] Share the draft canvas with the team for feedback; adjust based on their input.

**Phase 2: Compensation Pilot Design (Week 2–3)**  
- [ ] Fill out the Compensation Redesign Blueprint (Section 4).  
- [ ] Set metric targets and measurement methods.  
- [ ] Discuss individually with each team member: explain the pilot, safe harbor, and how their role is evolving.  
- [ ] Secure agreement (even if informal) to run the parallel compensation pilot for two quarters.

**Phase 3: Quality Protocol Installation (Week 3–4)**  
- [ ] Document the Human-in-the-Loop SOP (Section 5) and share with the team.  
- [ ] Run a dry-run quality check on a recent, completed engagement using the three checkpoints.  
- [ ] Adjust the SOP based on dry-run findings.

**Phase 4: Financial Simulation and Go-Live (Week 4–5)**  
- [ ] Complete the Integrated Financial Simulation (Section 6) for the first pilot engagement.  
- [ ] Ensure the VRR fee, engagement letter, and retraining conversation are ready (per earlier chapters).  
- [ ] Launch the pilot engagement under the new delivery model.  
- [ ] Start tracking quality log and performance metrics from day one.

**Phase 5: Review and Refine (After First Pilot, then Monthly)**  
- [ ] After the pilot engagement, gather team feedback and review quality log and metrics.  
- [ ] Compare actual margin to simulation; refine delivery cost estimates.  
- [ ] Adjust role metrics or compensation weights as needed.  
- [ ] Document lessons learned for the internal sell (Chapter 10).
