## 9. Post-Pyramid Delivery Quick-Reference Card

**The Three Layers (not seniority, function):**
- **Strategic Oversight (Partner):** Outcome definition, pricing, certification, escalation.  
- **AI-Augmented Analysis (Senior Professionals):** AI orchestration, judgment overlay, synthesis.  
- **Client-Embedded Validation (Client-Facing Professional):** Continuous client contact, feedback capture, implementation support.

**Compensation Principles:**  
- Replace billable-hour metrics with: outcome value generated, throughput velocity, AI orchestration quality, client outcome realization, lead generation.  
- Parallel pilot with safe harbor for two quarters.

**Quality Protocol (Three Checkpoints):**  
1. AI Output Validation – factual accuracy, relevance, completeness.  
2. Judgment Overlay – risk calibration, unknown unknowns, client nuance.  
3. Partner Certification – alignment with outcome definition, liability attachment.

**Financial Test:** Post-pyramid margin = VRR Fee – (Strategic Oversight cost + AI-Augmented Analysis cost + Client-Embedded cost + AI tools + overhead). Must exceed old hourly margin.

**Integration Reminder:** This delivery model is the internal completion of your audit, VRR engine, outcome packaging, and retraining conversations. If any pillar changes, revisit the delivery design. The system is one piece.
