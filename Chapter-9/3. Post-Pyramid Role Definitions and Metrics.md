## 3. Post-Pyramid Role Definitions and Metrics

Redefine what “good performance” means for each role in this service line. Use these templates to create role cards that can be shared with the team.

### Role: Strategic Oversight (Partner / Senior Partner)

- **Primary purpose:** Assure the outcome, price the risk, and own the client relationship at the strategic level.  
- **Key activities:** Outcome definition, retraining conversations, certification of deliverables, escalation of judgment calls.  
- **Performance metrics (replace billable hours):**  
  - Outcome value generated ($ amount of fees from outcome-based engagements)  
  - Realization rate on outcome-based fees (target: ≥95%)  
  - Client retention and expansion (repeat outcome engagements, new service lines opened)  
  - Certification quality (zero material errors or liability claims traceable to oversight failure)

### Role: AI-Augmented Analysis Professional (Senior Manager / Director)

- **Primary purpose:** Convert raw AI output into partner-ready, contextually calibrated analysis.  
- **Key activities:** AI prompting and validation, judgment overlay, preparation of synthesis with escalation flags.  
- **Performance metrics (replace utilization):**  
  - Throughput velocity: Average time from assignment to partner-ready synthesis (target: continually decreasing as AI tools improve)  
  - AI orchestration quality: Error rate caught before partner review, appropriate escalation of AI confidence gaps (measured by fewer partner corrections)  
  - Client impact contribution: Documented role in achieving defined outcomes (via feedback loop or client comments)

### Role: Client-Embedded Validation Professional (Manager / Associate with client skills)

- **Primary purpose:** Ensure the outcome is realized in the client’s environment and surface new opportunities.  
- **Key activities:** Day-to-day client communication, implementation check-ins, feedback capture, lead generation for future engagements.  
- **Performance metrics (replace utilization):**  
  - Outcome realization score: Client confirmation that the outcome was achieved as defined (binary or scaled)  
  - Client satisfaction (NPS or direct feedback, tracked per engagement)  
  - New outcome-based engagement leads generated from the relationship  
  - Timeliness and quality of feedback captured for pilot loop
