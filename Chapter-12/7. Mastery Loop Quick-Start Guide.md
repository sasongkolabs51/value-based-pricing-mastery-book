## 7. Mastery Loop Quick-Start Guide

If you do nothing else from this workbook, do these four things every quarter. They’ll keep your edge sharp.

- **Scan:** Spend 15 minutes reading about AI developments relevant to your practice. One trusted source is enough.  
- **Spot-check:** Look at two recent engagements. Did the client view the fee as fair? Did your margin hold?  
- **Adjust one thing:** Don’t overhaul. Tweak one VRR input, one script phrase, one packaging element.  
- **Test it:** On the very next engagement, apply the tweak and note the result.

That’s it. Four micro-actions, four times a year, and your system will evolve ahead of the market.
