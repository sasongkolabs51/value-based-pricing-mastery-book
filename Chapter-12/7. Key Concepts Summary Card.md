**THE MASTERY LOOP (Quarterly Rhythm)**  
1. **Scan:** AI landscape, client signals, internal data.  
2. **Recalibrate:** Adjust one audit score, VRR parameter, or script.  
3. **Pilot:** Test the adjustment on a single engagement.  
4. **Scale:** Integrate what works into templates and Council guidelines.

**THE INNOVATION RESERVE**  
- Embed 2–5% of every VRR fee into a notional fund.  
- Spend it on tools, conferences, protected time—guilt‑free.  
- It turns adaptation from a personal sacrifice into a line item.

**MENTORING AS MASTERY**  
- Commit to teaching one person through the full system each year.  
- Each session deepens your own understanding.  
- It builds a network that shifts the profession’s norms.

**LEGACY PRACTICES**  
- Weekly Lighthouse Reflection (Chapter 11).  
- Quarterly Pioneer Pulse (Chapter 11).  
- Annual Legacy Reflection (this chapter).  
- Track your Legacy Inventory over time.

**THE PERPETUAL EDGE FORMULA**  
*Mastery = System + Adaptation + Teaching.*  
You built the system. You now adapt it quarterly. You teach it to others. This loop is your lifetime insurance against any technology shift.

**GOLDEN RULE**  
*The goal is not to predict the next AI breakthrough. The goal is to have a system that absorbs any breakthrough into a pricing model that grows sharper, not weaker, with every advance.*
