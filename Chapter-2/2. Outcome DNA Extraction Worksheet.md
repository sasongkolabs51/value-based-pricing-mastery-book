**Step 3: Pressure‑Test Your Trio**  
For each of the three, ask yourself:

- *Is this result something clients can reliably get from any other single professional I know?* If the answer is yes, keep digging for something rarer.  
- *Can I point to at least two real‑world examples where I delivered this exact outcome?* If not, is there a variation you can name that is truly documented?  
- *Would a client pay a premium solely to secure this outcome, regardless of time spent?* This is the litmus test.
