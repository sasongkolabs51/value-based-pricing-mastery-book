## 6. Trusted Advisor to Value Architect Pivot Cheat Sheet  
*A quick‑reference card for when you catch yourself slipping back into the old identity language.*

| Instead of this (Trusted Advisor speak) | Say this to yourself (Value Architect mindset) |
|----------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| “I help clients with regulatory compliance.” | “I keep executives out of personal liability and their operations running.” |
| “We did a $50K review in one day.” | “We secured a clean compliance outcome that protected the client from a potential $2M penalty—delivered with total certainty.” |
| “My rate is $X per hour.” | “I price for the outcome of [specific risk avoidance / value creation], not the minutes.” |
| “I’m worried the client will think we overcharged.” | “The client paid to avoid a disaster that would have cost them 20x this fee. I need to remind myself—and them—of that.” |
| “I feel guilty that AI did the drafting.” | “AI revealed where my judgment adds the real value. That’s the part I’ll price.” |

Keep this sheet tucked in your notebook or saved on your phone. The old language is a habit, not a truth. Replace it intentionally until the new language becomes the default.
