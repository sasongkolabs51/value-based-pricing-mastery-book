## 3. Value Architect Manifesto Builder  
*Purpose: Craft a single‑sentence internal compass that realigns you every time the old identity tries to take over.*

**Structure:**  
*“I [transformational verb] [specific client type] to [measurable business outcome] by [unique capability].”*

- **Transformational verb:** Not “help,” “advise,” or “support.” Try “protect,” “unlock,” “accelerate,” “secure,” “shield,” “transform,” “resolve.”  
- **Specific client type:** Be as narrow as you can bear. Not “businesses,” but “mid‑market manufacturers,” “private equity operating partners,” “family‑owned food exporters.”  
- **Measurable business outcome:** Use the language of your Outcome DNA—penalties avoided, deals closed, risks transferred, revenues preserved.  
- **Unique capability:** The move only you make. It could be a methodology, a way of reading regulations, a forensic skill, a network.
