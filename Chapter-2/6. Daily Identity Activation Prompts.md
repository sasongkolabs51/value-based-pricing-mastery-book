## 6. Daily Identity Activation Prompts

These are quick, repeatable prompts to keep your Value Architect identity front-of-mind during ordinary workdays. Choose one each morning or rotate through them.

- *Morning question:* “What is one specific client outcome I can advance today that has nothing to do with hours spent?”  
- *Midday check:* “Am I measuring this afternoon’s progress by tasks completed or by client risk reduced?”  
- *Evening reframe:* “What’s one outcome I created today that would still be valuable at 10x the time it took?”  
- *Pre-meeting prompt:* “What is the disaster I’m here to prevent for this client, and how can I name it explicitly in the conversation?”  
- *Post-meeting reflection:* “Did I introduce myself as a trusted advisor, or as someone who secures a specific result?”

Write the one that resonates most on a sticky note and place it on your monitor. Repetition installs identity.
