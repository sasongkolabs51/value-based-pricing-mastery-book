## 11. Retraining Conversation Quick-Reference Card

**The Three-Question Redirect (in your back pocket):**  
1. “Can we confirm the outcome was delivered to your satisfaction?”  
2. “What was the business impact—what was at stake if this had gone wrong?”  
3. “Given that, does the fee feel like a reasonable investment in that certainty?”

**The Future-Pacing Close:**  
“For our next piece of work, I’d like to structure it the same way—define the outcome, fix the fee to the risk, no surprise invoices. Does that sound like a better way to work together?”

**The Core Principle:**  
Never defend the fee with hours. Always connect it to the risk transferred and the outcome achieved. The system you’ve built gives you the proof; this conversation simply makes it visible.

**When the Client Pushes Back:**  
You’re not arguing. You’re inviting them to compare the fee to the cost of the problem—and you have the numbers to make that comparison crystal clear.
