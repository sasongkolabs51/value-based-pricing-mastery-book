## Tool 5: Role-Play Practice Guide

*Use this with a trusted colleague. Set aside one hour, record the session, and debrief using the Scorecard (Tool 3).*

**Phase 1 – Script Drill (15 min)**  
- Partner A plays the skeptical client; Partner B plays the Value Architect.  
- Run through the Three-Question Redirect exactly as written.  
- Repeat until phrasing feels natural.

**Phase 2 – Objection Drill (20 min)**  
- The “client” raises the five objections one at a time, in random order.  
- The “partner” responds using the prepared evidence-backed scripts.  
- After each objection, pause and give brief feedback.

**Phase 3 – Live-Fire Simulation (25 min)**  
- The “client” throws curveballs: starts with a procurement objection, pivots to scope change, then circles back to overpayment fear—all in the same five-minute exchange.  
- The “partner” must navigate seamlessly and transition into the Future-Pacing Close.  
- Record the full simulation.

**Debrief**  
- Listen to the recording and fill out the Conversation Quality Scorecard.  
- Identify one specific improvement to work on before the next simulation.
