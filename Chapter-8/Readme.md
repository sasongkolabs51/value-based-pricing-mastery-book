# Chapter 8 Companion Workbook  
*The Retraining Conversation – Client Scripts That Permanently Shift Buying Criteria*

This workbook turns the chapter’s conversation architecture into a personal skill. You’ll build your own script library, practice the redirects until they become reflex, and equip yourself to handle any objection without retreating to the billable hour. By the end, you’ll have a customized conversation flow that integrates your audit, VRR engine, outcome packaging, and pilot data—so every retraining conversation feels like a natural extension of the system you’ve already built.
