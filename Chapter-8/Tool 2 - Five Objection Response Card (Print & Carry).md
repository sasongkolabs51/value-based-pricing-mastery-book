## Tool 2: Five Objection Response Card (Print & Carry)

*Compact version for your pocket or desk. Keep it visible during early live conversations.*

- **Overpay fear?** → Shift from “hours vs. fee” to “fee vs. risk exposure.” Use the at-risk number.
- **Procurement?** → Offer a business case summary instead of hours.
- **Scope change?** → Refer to the scope‑change protocol in the engagement letter.
- **Outcome failure?** → Refer to the value assurance mechanism; frame it as insurance.
- **Industry inertia?** → Cite Thomson Reuters data and your own pilot results.
