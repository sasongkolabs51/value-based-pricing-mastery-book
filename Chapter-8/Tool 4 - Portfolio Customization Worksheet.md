**Customized Three-Question Redirect**
1. “Before we discuss the fee mechanics, can we confirm that [outcome] was delivered to your satisfaction?”
2. “If I may, what was the business impact—what was at stake if [outcome] hadn’t been achieved?”
3. “Given that outcome and the $[at-risk value] risk it neutralized, does the $[VRR fee] fee feel like a reasonable investment in that certainty?”

**Customized Future-Pacing Close**  
“For [next engagement], I’d propose the same outcome‑based structure. You’d have budget certainty, and we’d be fully aligned on the result. Does that sound like a better way to work together going forward?”
