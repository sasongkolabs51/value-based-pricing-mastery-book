**Step 2: Prioritize Your Engagement Sequence**

- **Phase 1 (Before the big meeting):** Meet individually with early adopters and the most influential fence‑sitter. Share your pilot data, ask for input, invite them to co‑sponsor the Charter. Secure at least one visible ally who will speak up in the meeting.  
- **Phase 2 (During the big meeting):** Target the presentation to the fence‑sitters. Acknowledge blockers’ concerns without letting them dominate. Use early adopters’ voices to create social proof.  
- **Phase 3 (After the big meeting):** Follow up with fence‑sitters one‑on‑one. Provide a simple “first step” offer—help them run a diagnostic on one of their engagements. Ignore blockers until momentum makes their resistance untenable.
