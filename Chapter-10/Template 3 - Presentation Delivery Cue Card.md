## Template 3: Presentation Delivery Cue Card (“Never Defend; Always Redirect”)

**Purpose:** Keep this card visible during the Q&A. When a partner challenges the model, use these prompts to redirect rather than defend.

- **Compensation challenge:** “That’s exactly why I designed the compensation pilot—so we can test this without touching anyone’s base draw. Let me walk you through the hold‑harmless structure again.”
- **Client acceptance challenge:** “I had the same concern before I ran the pilot. Here’s what the client actually said after we delivered…” [show verbatim slide]
- **‘It won’t work for my practice’ challenge:** “I’d be happy to sit with you and map one of your matters through the audit framework. We’ll see exactly where your judgment intensity and risk transfer scores land. Would you be open to that?”
- **Market valuation challenge:** “The market rewards margins and client stickiness. Our outcome‑based engagements are showing higher margins and deeper client relationships. That’s a better story for valuation than declining realization rates.”
