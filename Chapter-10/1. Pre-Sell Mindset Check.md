## 1. Pre-Sell Mindset Check

Before you build a single slide, check your own readiness to lead this conversation. Tick any that apply.

- [ ] I’m nervous that my partners will see this as a threat to their compensation, not an opportunity.
- [ ] I haven’t gathered the firm-wide data I’d need to make a burning platform credible to everyone.
- [ ] I’m not sure I can answer the question “What happens to partner draws during the transition?” without hesitation.
- [ ] I worry that blockers—partners whose books are heavily commoditized—will torpedo the conversation.
- [ ] I haven’t identified my executive committee sponsor or early adopter coalition yet.
- [ ] I’ve never presented a formal business case for firm-wide change; I’m more comfortable talking about client work.
- [ ] Part of me still wonders if this will really work at scale, and that doubt might leak into my presentation.

If you ticked any, you’re normal. This workbook is designed to fill those gaps methodically, so that by the time you present, you’ll be answering questions from a place of evidence, not hope.
