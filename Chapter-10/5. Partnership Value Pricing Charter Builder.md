**PARTNERSHIP VALUE PRICING CHARTER**  
**[Firm Name]**  
**Effective Date: [Insert Date]**

**1. PREAMBLE & PRINCIPLES**  
[Firm Name] formally commits to transitioning toward client‑aligned, AI‑proof pricing models that tie professional fees to business outcomes and risk absorption, rather than solely to input time. We believe this shift is essential to preserve our competitive position, protect partner income, and deliver superior client value in an AI‑augmented market.

**2. GOVERNANCE: THE VALUE PRICING COUNCIL**  
A Value Pricing Council is hereby established, comprising [Number] partners appointed by the Executive Committee. The Council’s responsibilities include:  
- Overseeing the standards and methodology for outcome‑based pricing engagements.  
- Capturing and reporting pilot data (realization rates, client satisfaction, margin analysis).  
- Mentoring and supporting partners who opt into the pilot program.  
- Reporting to the Executive Committee quarterly on progress and recommendations.

**Initial Council Members:** [Names / Roles]

**3. THE OPT‑IN PILOT PROGRAM**  
A 12‑month pilot program is authorized, commencing [Start Date] and concluding [End Date].  
- **Participation:** Any partner may opt in for eligible engagements, as defined by the Council.  
- **Compensation Hold‑Harmless:** Participating partners will maintain their base draw at current levels. A variable component of partner compensation, up to [X]% of the individual partner’s draw, will be tied to three outcome‑based metrics: Outcome Value Originated, Outcome Realization Rate, and Client Retention & Expansion within outcome‑based engagements. No partner will see a reduction in base draw due to participation in the pilot.  
- **Support:** The Council will provide templates, retraining scripts, and individual coaching to all participating partners.

**4. REVIEW & ADOPTION TIMELINE**  
The Executive Committee will conduct a formal review of pilot data on [Review Date, ~12 months out]. Based on the evidence, the partnership will vote on:  
- Permanent adoption of the Value Pricing framework as a core firm offering.  
- Integration of the Value Pricing Council into the firm’s ongoing governance structure.  
- Adjustments to the partner compensation system to reflect outcome‑based revenue at scale.
