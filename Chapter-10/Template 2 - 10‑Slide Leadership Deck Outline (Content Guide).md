## Template 2: 10‑Slide Leadership Deck Outline (Content Guide)

Use this structured outline to build your presentation. Each slide includes the core message, suggested data, and a brief speaker note.

**Slide 1 – Title & Framing**
- *Title:* Building a Post‑Billable Future: Why Our Firm Must Lead
- *Speaker note:* “Today I’ll show you a threat we can’t ignore, a solution I’ve already tested, and a path we can walk together—with no risk to your current draw.”

**Slide 2 – The External Threat**
- *Headline:* “72% of Law Firm Leaders Believe AI Will Force a Change in the Billable Hour Within 5 Years” (Thomson Reuters 2025)
- *Bullets:* AI is not coming; it’s here. Competitors are experimenting. Clients are questioning invoices that feel too fast.
- *Speaker note:* “This is a measurable shift. It’s accelerating.”

**Slide 3 – Our Firm’s Reality**
- *Headline:* “Our Realization Rate Has Fallen from 85% to 76% in 3 Years”
- *Visual:* Line chart of firm‑wide (or your practice’s) realization over time.
- *Speaker note:* “Every point of realization we lose is profit we’ve earned but can’t collect.”

**Slide 4 – The Cost of Inaction**
- *Headline:* “If This Trend Continues, Profit Per Partner Will Contract by X% Within 24 Months”
- *Data:* Red‑zone revenue leakage projection.
- *Speaker note:* “This is not hypothetical. It’s arithmetic.”

**Slide 5 – The Broken Leverage Model**
- *Headline:* “AI Efficiency Is Hollowing Out Our Traditional Pyramid”
- *Visual:* Old pyramid (partner → juniors) crumbling as AI eliminates junior hours.
- *Speaker note:* “When leverage disappears, we must find a new economic model or watch income decline.”

**Slide 6 – The Proof: Our Pilot Results**
- *Headline:* “Outcome‑Based Pricing Delivered a Higher Margin Than the Billable Hour”
- *Data:* Pilot realization rate (100%), client satisfaction score, client verbatim quote, and margin comparison chart (old vs. new).
- *Speaker note:* “This is not a theory. I did this with our client, with our team, and here are the numbers.”

**Slide 7 – How the Model Works**
- *Headline:* “The Value Realization Rate Engine – Pricing the Outcome, Not the Effort”
- *Visual:* One‑page VRR diagram (OS → RA → JI → Fee) from Chapter 5.
- *Speaker note:* “The fee is tied to the risk we absorb and the outcome we guarantee. AI speed makes it more profitable.”

**Slide 8 – The Compensation Answer**
- *Headline:* “Opt‑In Pilot: Maintain Your Base Draw. Earn More for Delivering Outcomes.”
- *Bullets:* 12‑month opt‑in pilot. Base draw untouched. Variable comp tied to outcome value, realization, and client retention.
- *Speaker note:* “No one’s draw is at risk. You can opt in, or you can wait and see. The data will speak.”

**Slide 9 – The Path Forward: The Charter**
- *Headline:* “Proposed: Partnership Value Pricing Charter”
- *Visual:* The one‑page Charter framework (Preamble, Council, Pilot Program, Review Timeline).
- *Speaker note:* “This Charter creates a governed, low‑risk way to move forward together.”

**Slide 10 – Call to Action**
- *Headline:* “I’m Asking for Three Things Today”
- *Bullets:* 1) Approve the Charter. 2) Authorize the opt‑in pilot cohort. 3) Commit to a review in 12 months.
- *Speaker note:* “The market isn’t waiting. I need a named sponsor and a date for the first Council meeting. Who will step forward with me?”
