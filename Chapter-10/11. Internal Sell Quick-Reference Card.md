## 11. Internal Sell Quick-Reference Card

**The Three-Act Structure:**  
1. **Burning Platform:** External threat + internal realization decline + cost of inaction.  
2. **Proof:** Your pilot results—realization, margin, client quotes, margin comparison.  
3. **Path:** Compensation pilot, political strategy, charter, and governance.

**The One Sentence You Must Be Able to Say Without Hesitation:**  
*“The billable hour is costing us real money right now, and we have a tested alternative that improves margins, client relationships, and talent retention—starting with an opt-in pilot that protects everyone’s compensation during the transition.”*

**The Only Defense Is Redirection:**  
When challenged, don’t defend. Invite. *“Let’s run a diagnostic on one of your engagements and see what the data says.”*

**The Charter Is Your Institutional Legacy:**  
It’s the document that makes the transition bigger than you. Without it, you’re a lone voice. With it, you’ve built a mechanism that outlasts any single meeting.
