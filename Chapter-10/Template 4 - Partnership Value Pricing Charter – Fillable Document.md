# PARTNERSHIP VALUE PRICING CHARTER  
**Adopting the Post‑Billable Model**

*This one‑page charter transforms individual success into an institutional mechanism.*

## 1. PREAMBLE & PRINCIPLES

**Commitment**  
[Insert firm name] formally commits to transitioning toward client‑aligned, AI‑proof pricing models that tie professional fees to business outcomes and risk absorption, rather than input time.

**Guiding Principles**  
- **Client Alignment:** Fees reflect the value created and the risk absorbed, not the time spent.  
- **Transparency:** Engagement letters define success criteria upfront, eliminating invoice surprises.  
- **Sustainability:** Our economic model must reward speed, accuracy, and client impact, ensuring long‑term profitability and talent retention.

## 2. GOVERNANCE: THE VALUE PRICING COUNCIL

**Establishment**  
A Value Pricing Council is hereby created to oversee the firm’s transition.

**Membership**  
The Council shall consist of [Number] partners, including:  
- [Name], [Role/Practice Group]  
- [Name], [Role/Practice Group]  
- [Name], [Role/Practice Group]  

**Responsibilities**  
- Approve pilot engagements and review outcome‑based fee proposals  
- Capture pilot data (realization, client satisfaction, margin)  
- Maintain and update the firm’s retraining scripts and packaging templates  
- Mentor participating partners  
- Report quarterly to the Executive Committee  

## 3. THE OPT‑IN PILOT PROGRAM

**Duration**  
12 months, commencing on [Date].

**Eligibility**  
Any equity partner may voluntarily opt into the pilot by notifying the Value Pricing Council and completing the Pilot Blueprint for at least one eligible engagement.

**Compensation Hold‑Harmless**  
Participating partners will maintain their base draw at current levels. A variable component (up to [X]% of base draw) will be tied to the following outcome‑based metrics:  
- Outcome Value Originated (total VRR‑based fees closed)  
- Outcome Realization Rate (percentage of those fees collected without discount)  
- Client Retention & Expansion (within outcome‑based engagements)

**Metrics for Success**  
Pilot engagements will be evaluated on:  
- Realization rate recovery  
- Client satisfaction scores  
- Post‑pyramid margin expansion  

## 4. REVIEW & ADOPTION TIMELINE

**Review Date**  
The Executive Committee will review aggregated pilot data on [Date, 12 months from pilot start].

**Decision**  
Based on the data, the partnership will vote on:  
- Broader adoption of the Value Pricing model across the firm  
- Permanent integration of the Value Pricing Council into firm governance  

**Accountable Officer**  
[Name], [Title], is accountable for convening the review and presenting the data.
