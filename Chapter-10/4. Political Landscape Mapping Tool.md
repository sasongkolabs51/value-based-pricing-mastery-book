## 4. Political Landscape Mapping Tool

Before you present, you need to know who’s in the room—and what they care about. Map the key influencers. Use one sheet per significant partner or group.

**Key:**  
- Early Adopter: Already experimenting, frustrated with billable hour, will visibly support.  
- Fence-Sitter: Curious but risk-averse; needs data and a low-risk entry point.  
- Blocker: Vested in hourly model; likely to oppose publicly. Strategy: neutralize, don’t convert—contain influence by building a strong coalition.
