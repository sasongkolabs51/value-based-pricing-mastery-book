### Engagement Strategy Notes

**Early Adopters**
- Share templates, pilot data, and the Charter draft.
- Invite them to co‑present or publicly endorse the initiative.
- Ask them to mentor one fence‑sitter each.

**Fence‑Sitters**
- Offer a low‑risk entry point: a single‑engagement pilot with a compensation hold‑harmless.
- Walk them through the VRR engine using their own numbers.
- Provide the retraining scripts and offer to role‑play the conversation.

**Blockers**
- Do not attempt direct conversion.
- Contain influence by building a visible coalition of early adopters.
- Secure Executive Committee sponsorship to provide top cover.
