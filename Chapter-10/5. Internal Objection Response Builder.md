### Objection 1: “How do we determine partner compensation without billable hours?”

**Response script:**  
“That’s the central question, and I’ve built a pilot structure to answer it. For the pilot period, partners keep their base draw. The variable component is tied to three metrics: outcome value originated, realization rate on outcome-based fees, and client retention. These are more stable than hourly realization because they’re anchored to client value, not hour compression. After 12 months, we’ll have real data on how these metrics perform—and we’ll adjust together.”
