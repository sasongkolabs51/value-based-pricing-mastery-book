## 9. Post-Billable Leader Quick-Reference Card

**Your Story (4 Acts):** Crisis → Insight → Proof → Invitation. Keep it ready for any conversation.

**The Council:** 3–5 partners, chartered by executive committee, meeting monthly. Govern standards, capture data, mentor others, report.

**The Public Proof:** One major piece of writing, one speaking engagement, one mentoring commitment per year, minimum. Repurpose everything.

**Handling Doubt (Internal):** When the billable-hour voice whispers, open your Doubt Detox Log. Find the evidence. Reframe. You are not your doubts; you are the system that disproves them.

**The Maintenance Rhythm:**  
- Weekly: Lighthouse Reflection (20 min Friday)  
- Quarterly: Pioneer Pulse (self-assessment)  
- Annually: Thought Leadership Calendar + Legacy Inventory update

**The Deeper Identity:** You are no longer a partner who bills time. You are a Pricing Pioneer. Your value is the outcomes you secure, the leaders you develop, and the certainty you bring to a profession in transition. Every time you share your story, you crack the old model a little more—and open space for a new one.
