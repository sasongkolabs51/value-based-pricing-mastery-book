**1. MISSION**  
The Value Pricing Council exists to accelerate [Firm Name]’s transition from time‑based billing to outcome‑based, client‑aligned pricing models. We will ensure this shift is disciplined, data‑driven, and profitable—protecting our client relationships, our partner economics, and our competitive position.

The Council will elect a Chair annually. The Chair reports to the Executive Committee.

**3. RESPONSIBILITIES**  
The Council has four core responsibilities:

- **Govern Standards:** Maintain and update the AI‑Susceptibility Audit framework, the VRR engine guidelines, outcome packaging templates, retraining scripts, and post‑pyramid delivery model. Ensure consistency and quality across all outcome‑based engagements.  
- **Capture Data:** Aggregate pilot results firm‑wide—realization rates, client satisfaction scores, margin comparisons, and talent retention metrics. Publish a quarterly dashboard to the partnership.  
- **Mentor Participants:** Each Council member will directly support 2‑3 early‑adopter partners through their first pilot. Support includes co‑designing the outcome statement, reviewing the VRR calculation, rehearsing the retraining conversation, and debriefing after engagement close.  
- **Report to Executive Committee:** Provide quarterly updates on adoption metrics, financial impact, and any risks. Recommend adjustments to compensation design, template language, or governance as evidence accumulates.

**4. QUARTERLY REPORTING CADENCE**  
The Council will deliver a written dashboard and a 30‑minute verbal briefing to the Executive Committee each quarter, covering:  
- Number of active outcome‑based engagements and total outcome value priced.  
- Aggregate realization rate on those engagements vs. firm average.  
- Client satisfaction and retention data.  
- Partner participation and any compensation pilot results.  
- Key lessons learned and recommended adjustments.

**5. REVIEW & RENEWAL**  
The Charter will be reviewed annually by the Executive Committee. Adjustments to membership, responsibilities, or the compensation pilot will be made based on the evidence collected.
