**Step 3: Anonymization checklist.**  
Before sharing any client data externally, ensure:  
- [ ] Client name and industry specifics are removed or sufficiently generalized.  
- [ ] Exact dollar figures are rounded or expressed as percentages.  
- [ ] Quotes are paraphrased unless written permission is obtained.  
- [ ] The story does not inadvertently reveal the client’s identity through context clues.  
- [ ] A trusted colleague reviews the piece to catch any unintended identification.
