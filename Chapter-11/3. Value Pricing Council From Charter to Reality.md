### Step 2: First Council Meeting Agenda Template

- **Welcome and purpose (5 min):** Reaffirm charter, role of the council.  
- **Data review (15 min):** Share aggregated pilot results (realization, client feedback, margin).  
- **Standards update (15 min):** Discuss any refinements to audit, VRR engine guidelines, or packaging templates based on recent learnings.  
- **Mentorship assignments (10 min):** Identify next wave of pilot partners and assign council mentors.  
- **Executive committee report prep (10 min):** Decide key metrics and stories to include in quarterly report.  
- **Next steps and date (5 min):** Set next meeting, action items.
