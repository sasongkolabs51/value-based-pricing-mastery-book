## 5. Leadership Resilience & Maintenance Protocols  
*Purpose: Build daily, weekly, and quarterly practices that keep your leadership grounded, adaptable, and energized.*

---

**A. Weekly Lighthouse Reflection (20 minutes every Friday)**

Use this simple journal prompt:

- *What was my most significant leadership moment this week—a conversation, a decision, a visible act?*  
- *Where did I feel the pull back to the old billable‑hour identity, and how did I respond?*  
- *What one piece of evidence (client quote, margin number, colleague’s feedback) most affirms my path right now?*  
- *What is my single leadership intention for next week?*

**B. Quarterly Pioneer Pulse (90 minutes, every quarter)**

Set aside time at the end of each quarter to review your leadership trajectory. Use this structured agenda:

1. **Review your Legacy Inventory** (see Section 6). Update the numbers.  
2. **Re‑read your Pricing Perspective.** Does it still ring true? Add a sentence reflecting the quarter’s most important lesson.  
3. **Assess your Council’s progress.** What’s working? Where is resistance still showing up? Adjust your mentoring approach.  
4. **Review your Public Sharing Planner.** Did you hit your publishing commitments? What responses did you receive?  
5. **Set one stretch leadership goal** for the next quarter (e.g., speak at a conference, mentor a new partner outside your practice, submit an article to a top‑tier publication).  
6. **Celebrate.** Write down three specific wins from the quarter. Do not skip this step.
