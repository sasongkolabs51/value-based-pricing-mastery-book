# VALUE PRICING COUNCIL CHARTER

**Preamble**  
The [Firm Name] partners hereby establish the Value Pricing Council to govern the transition from billable‑hour pricing to outcome‑aligned, AI‑proof commercial models. The Council’s authority is delegated by the Executive Committee and is subject to annual review.

**1. Council Membership**
- **Size:** The Council shall consist of [Number] partners.
- **Founding Members:** [Name], [Practice/Office]; [Name], [Practice/Office]; [Name], [Practice/Office]; [Name], [Practice/Office]; [Name], [Practice/Office].
- **Chair:** [Name] shall serve as the initial Chair for a term of [12 months / 2 years].
- **Succession:** New members are nominated by the Council and confirmed by the Executive Committee.

**2. Responsibilities**
- **Standards & Templates:** Maintain and update the firm’s AI‑Susceptibility Audit, VRR Engine guidelines, Outcome Packaging templates, and Retraining Scripts.
- **Data Aggregation:** Collect and analyze pilot results—realization rates, client satisfaction, margin comparisons—and report quarterly to the Executive Committee.
- **Mentorship:** Each Council member serves as a mentor to at least [Number] early‑adopter partners, guiding them through their first outcome‑based pilot.
- **Quality Assurance:** Oversee the Human‑in‑the‑Loop quality protocol and the post‑pyramid delivery model to ensure consistent, premium‑grade output.
- **Market Positioning:** Coordinate the firm’s external thought leadership on value pricing, including articles, conference presentations, and client roundtables.

**3. Pilot Program Parameters**
- **Duration:** The Council shall oversee a formal pilot phase of [12 months], commencing [Date].
- **Opt‑In Eligibility:** Any equity partner may opt in by submitting a Pilot Blueprint to the Council for approval.
- **Compensation Hold‑Harmless:** Participating partners retain their current base draw. A variable component tied to outcome value originated, outcome realization rate, and client retention/expansion will be added.
- **Metrics for Success:** Pilot engagements will be evaluated on realization rate recovery, client satisfaction, post‑pyramid margin expansion, and talent retention.

**4. Reporting & Governance**
- **Quarterly Dashboard:** The Council shall present a dashboard of adoption metrics, financial results, and client feedback to the Executive Committee.
- **Annual Review:** No later than [Date, 12 months after pilot start], the partnership will review the aggregate data and vote on permanent integration of value pricing into firm governance and partner compensation.
